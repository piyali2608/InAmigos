About Us

Building an Inclusive, Compassionate India

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered, not-for-profit organization founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). Based in Chhattisgarh, we create a more inclusive, compassionate, and empowered society through structured, impact-driven projects.

From over 50,000+ meals distributed under Project Seva to bridging digital literacy gaps through Project Bachpanshala — every initiative creates lasting change at the grassroots level.

Our Projects

Six Pillars of Change

Each initiative addresses a critical social need — together they form a holistic ecosystem of care and empowerment.

Basic Needs 🍱

Project Seva

Providing food and clothing to underprivileged communities. Over 50,000+ meals served including during COVID-19 relief across India.

Education 📚

Project Bachpanshala

Bridging the learning gap through tutoring, digital literacy, and mentoring — ensuring every underprivileged child gets quality education.

Animal Welfare 🐾

Project Jeev

Rescue, protection, and feeding of street animals. Medical care and community campaigns promoting humane treatment and responsible ownership.

✊
Empowerment ✊

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development, financial literacy, and income-generating opportunities — creating a ripple effect that uplifts entire families.

🌳
Environment 🌳

Project Prakriti

Tree plantation, waste management, water conservation, and climate awareness campaigns — protecting the planet one community at a time.

🛠️
Livelihood 🛠️

Project Vikas

Vocational training and skill-development programs for youth and adults — building market-ready portfolios connecting to employment and self-employment.

Mission & Vision

Our North Star

🎯 Our Mission

Serve Humanity With Compassion

InAmigos Foundation brings positive, lasting change at the grassroots level. We believe real transformation begins with addressing basic needs and empowering communities to shape their own futures.

"Real transformation begins at the grassroots — one compassionate act at a time."
🌟 Our Vision

An Inclusive, Resilient, Sustainable India

We envision a society where every child, woman, and animal is treated with dignity, every ecosystem is protected, and every individual has access to opportunities for growth and self-reliance.

"Through collaboration, innovation, and community-driven action, we build a better India — together."
